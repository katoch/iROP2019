Invitation

Dear Colleagues,

It gives us immense pleasure to invite you for the 27'" Annual Conference of Vitreo Retinal Society of India scheduled from 29th November to 2nd December, Jaipur.

A Royal treat awaits you. The salubrious weather, the heart warming hospitality and the sumptuous cuisine will add vigour to the rich scientific fare. Jaipur, a unique historical city of India, is at its best at the onset of winter.

We are expecting more than 600 delegates from India and abroad. VRSI 2018, the biggest platform for Vitreo Retinal surgeons in India, promises a rich scientific fare. Jaipur is one of the most vibrant and sought after tourist destinations of India. The city of victory- originally known as Sawai Jaipur- is named after its founder, SawaiJai Singh. It is a mix of traditional and modern, surrounded by forts and palaces and dotted with enchanting gardens and famous temples. The city offers a rich experience of its heritage, its culture, arts and crafts, jewellery, fabric, shoes and much more.

We are having Retinal Imaging Symposium in the Hills of India (RISHI) on 29'" Nov.2018 at Hotel Le Meridien Resort & Spa, kukas, Jaipur

We extend our warm welcome to the delegates and promise you a most memorable conference at Jaipur.


Dr. Pawan Shorey

Organizing Chairman

Dr. R.K. Sharma

Organizing Secretary
