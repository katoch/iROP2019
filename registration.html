Mode of Payment
  • Trainees should enclose bonafide certificate from Head of Department / Institution along with the registration form.
  • In case of bank transfer, kindly email your transfer details along with the filled registration form
  • Demand Draft/Multicity Cheque to be drawn in favour of "VRSI Jaipur" payable at Jaipur. Please send duly filled registration form along with payment i.e. DD/Multicity Cheque to Conference secretariat.
  • Accompanying Person: For Spouse and Children (above 5 years) registration is mandatory.
  • The registration fee along with duly filled form must reach the conference secretariat on or before the deadline date, after which the next registration slab shall be applicable.
  • Registration fees will be based on the date of receipt of payment.
  • Conference Organizers are not responsible for postal delays.

Registration Cancellation and Refund Policy:
  • All cancellation should be made in writing and sent to the Conference Secretariat.
  • All cancellation received on or before 1st Sept. 2018 will be entitled for 50% refund of the registration amount paid.
  • No refund for cancellation made after 1st Sept. 2018
  • The refund process will be completed within 45 days of completion of the conference
